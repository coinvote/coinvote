Welcome to CoinVote.

CoinVote is a site where the 'shareholders' of Bitcoin can express their opinions about important topics affecting the bitcoin community and protocol. Satoshi's original vision was that proof-of-work would solve the problem of determining representation in majority decision making (https://bitcoin.org/bitcoin.pdf). Unfortunately, with the invention of ASICs, mining pools, and mining farms, the users of bitcoin have had their voting powers stripped from them. Miner's can voice their opinions on important changes by running different software or including messages in the coinbase transaction, but it is incredibly difficult to understand how the shareholders of Bitcoin feel through the noise and censorship of communities like r/Bitcoin.

What is CoinVote?

CoinVote is a way for holders of bitcoin to express their opinions on a wide variety of topics. We believe that the owners of bitcoin are the most important contingent in the bitcoin ecosystem and we want their voices to be heard. We believe in a 'one Satoshi-one vote' policy. A sort of 'proof-of-stake' methodology for policy decision making.
